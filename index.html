Adobe Premiere Pro handles long-form, multi-cam, and narrative work. CapCut Pro supports fast social workflows, alongside AI tools such as Claude and Higgsfield where they add real production value.

Short-form work generally takes 24–48 hours. YouTube edits take 3–5 business days, while documentaries and podcasts usually take 5–10 business days depending on footage and complexity.
