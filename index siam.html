FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before we start working together.

What software tools do you use for post-production?
+

I rely primarily on Adobe Premiere Pro for intricate multi-cam edits, long-form pacing, and advanced color grading. For snappy modern social shorts, I integrate CapCut Pro combined with specialized AI tools (Claude, Higgsfield, and more) to minimize workflow delays while maximizing output quality.

What is your typical turnaround time?
+

Short-form content (Reels, TikToks, Shorts): 24-48 hours per video.

YouTube long-form (up to 10 mins): 3-5 business days.

Documentaries & Podcasts: 5-10 business days depending on complexity.

Rush delivery available for an additional fee. I always communicate realistic timelines upfront so you can plan your content calendar.

How do revisions work? What if I don't like the first cut?
+

Every package includes revision rounds (2-3 depending on tier). Here's how it works:

  • 1. I deliver the first draft based on your brief and reference videos.
  • 2. You provide timestamped feedback via Google Docs or Loom.
  • 3. I implement changes and send the updated version.

Still not satisfied? I offer a 100% money-back guarantee on the first draft if we're not aligned on creative direction. Your satisfaction is my priority.

How do we communicate during the project?
+

I believe in over-communication to avoid surprises:

  • Slack / Discord for real-time chat and quick updates.
  • Frame.io for video feedback and annotations.
  • Google Drive / Dropbox for asset sharing.
  • Weekly check-in calls for long-term projects.

You'll never wonder about project status — I send progress updates at every milestone.

What file formats and resolutions do you deliver?
+

All videos are delivered in MP4 (H.264) optimized for your platform:

  • YouTube: 4K or 1080p, 24/30/60fps as requested.
  • Instagram/TikTok: 1080x1920 (9:16), optimized file size.
  • Podcasts: 1080p with audiogram waveform versions.

Need a specific format? Just ask — I can deliver in ProRes, MOV, or any custom spec your workflow requires.

What are your payment terms? Do you require upfront payment?
+

For new clients: 50% upfront, 50% on delivery. This protects both of us and ensures commitment.

For retainer/ongoing partnerships: Net-15 or monthly invoicing available.
